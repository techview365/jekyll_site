Funding

Gnoppix is a free software project. You can support its development by leaving a small donation.

How to donate

Here are the official channels from which you can donate to Gnoppix Linux Developers.

  • GitHub Sponsors

    By subscribing for only 2$ you can help keep the project alive. You can also make a one-time donation.

  • Ko-Fi

    You can also decide for an even more flexible subscription with Ko-Ki.

  • Patreon

    At the moment, Patreon only offers recurring memberships, and one-time donations are not available

About

Gnoppix Linux is a free and open-source operating system that is designed to be a faster, more powerful, secure, and privacy-respecting alternative to Windows and macOS.
