Gnoppix for gaming too!

Play on Linux just like you do on Windows!

Download

All the gaming platforms you already know, right here

The most popular gaming platforms (Epic Games Store, GOG Galaxy, Origin, Battle.net, EA Games..) are already available as an installer in Gnoppix. You don't have to do more than choose what you want and install it, just like you would on Windows!

Create a gaming bottle, install the stores you want and start playing right away!

Available installers

New PC? Bring your gaming bottle with you!

Whether you change your PC or just want to try a new Linux distribution, always carry your gaming bottle with you!

Create a backup or copy of your bottle at any time, to an external drive, a NAS ... and import it into a fresh installation to get back in action right away!

About

Gnoppix Linux is a free and open-source operating system that is designed to be a faster, more powerful, secure, and privacy-respecting alternative to Windows and macOS.
